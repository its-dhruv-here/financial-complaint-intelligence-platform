# Screenshot Capture Instructions

Capture the following screenshots from the running Streamlit app and save them in this directory to populate the README.

## 1. `routing.png` — Complaint Routing Tab

1. Run `streamlit run app.py`.
2. Stay on the **Complaint Routing** tab.
3. Select the "Mortgage Issue" example from the dropdown.
4. Click **Classify & Route**.
5. Capture the full page showing:
   - The sidebar with model metrics.
   - The routing decision card with the confidence score.
   - The top-3 probability bar chart.
   - The explainability panel (positive and negative keywords + coefficient chart).

## 2. `analytics.png` — Model Analytics Tab

1. Click the **Model Analytics** tab.
2. Capture the full page showing:
   - The six KPI cards (Accuracy, Precision, Recall, etc.).
   - The dataset overview metrics.
   - The class distribution chart.
   - The confusion matrix.

## 3. `benchmarks.png` — Benchmark Comparison Tab

1. Click the **Benchmark Comparison** tab.
2. Capture the full page showing:
   - The highlighted production model banner.
   - The styled benchmark data table.
   - The two comparison charts (Accuracy/F1 and All Metrics).
